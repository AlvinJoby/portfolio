About Me

I’m basically a full-time eater and a part-time everything else. Currently on a mission to gain weight—because apparently, 50kg isn’t enough to keep me from being blown away by strong winds.
I have a talent for making plans I may or may not follow, an unmatched ability to find food in any situation, and a deep appreciation for people who share their snacks.
When I’m not doing anything (which is rare), you’ll find me thinking about my next meal, avoiding unnecessary cardio, and wondering why mosquitoes find me so attractive.
If you made it this far, congrats—you’re either really bored or just as cool as me. Either way, I respect it!
